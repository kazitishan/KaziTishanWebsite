Lotto Luncheonette Candy . Deli

369 McDonald Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11218

I come here almost everyday to get a sandwich. My usual order is a hot turkey roll with lettuce, tomato, and American cheese

Pier 5

I come here to play soccer with my friends all the time and I have so many memories here. The sunset here is always beautiful.

Floyd Bennett Field

3159 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11234

I come here and watch the sunset with other people whenever it is empty. It is an extremely calming place and is one of my escapes from the city

City Hall Park

Broadway &, Chambers St, New York, NY 10007

I used to come to this part all the time during my internship and it brings back many fun memories I had over the summer. There are benches you can sit and relax to enjoy the nature

Parade Grounds

Coney Island Ave/Church Av, Parkside Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11218

I used to come here all the time with my dad and uncles to play soccer. It is not too far from my house and I still come here to play sometimes with my friends
