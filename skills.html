Rubik's Cube

I can solve any cube sized Rubik's cube. I learned to solve the Rubik's cube in fifth grade after being fascinated by how fast people were able to solve the puzzle

Follow the steps below to be able to solve a 5x5 Rubik's cube

Step Description
1 Align the center pieces of each side of the cube
2 Align every edge piece
3 Make a cross aligned to its according center
4 Solve for the first two layers
5 Solve for the last layer
